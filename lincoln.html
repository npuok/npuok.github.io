About Lincoln

Lincoln is the capital city of Nebraska. Although, it is the capital it is not the largest city. It's the second most populous city in the state of Nebraska following, Omaha. It is located in Lancaster county.

Huskers

Nebraska Football

Lincoln Nebraska is home to the Nebraska Huskers. The Huskers is the football team at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln.

    Fun Facts about the Huskers

  1. The Huskers have been playing since 1923. They started all the way home in Memorial stadium.
  2. The Huskers have had 400 consecutive sellouts since 1962.
  3. The Huskers have two mascots, Herbie Husker since 1974. The second one being Lil' Red since 1994.
