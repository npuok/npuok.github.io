About Omaha

Omaha is the largest city in the state of Nebraska. Although, it is the most populous city, Lincoln is actually the capital city of Nebraska. It is located in Douglas county.

What to See!

Henry Doorly Zoo & Aquarium

The Henry Doorly Zoo is one of the world's largest zoo in the world. There are a lot of different exhibits to see at the Omaha Zoo. Some of the exhibits are inside and others are outdoors. Some of the most popular exhibits being:

  1. Scott African Grasslands
  2. Lied Jungle
  3. Hubbard Expedition Madagascar
  4. Owen Sea Lion Shores
  5. Desert Dome
  6. Mahoney Kingdoms of the Night
  7. Bearniece Grewcock Butterfly and Insect Pavillion
Hours

The zoo is open all year round. Depending on the time of year, the hours will vary. The current zoo hours between November 4 to March 31 are from 10 AM to 4PM daily.

These are the current daily rates of general admission to the zoo.
