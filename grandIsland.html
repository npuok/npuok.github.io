About Grand Island

Grand Island is the fourth most populous city in the state of Nebraska. It is located in Hall county.

General Information
Population: 52,622
Year Incorporated: 1872
Region: Southeastern
Classification: Urban
Average Income: $59,061 (Below Average)

Nebraska State Fair

Grand Island is home to the Nebraska state fair. Nebraska's state fair was originally located in Lincoln. People from all over Nebraska come every to the state fair. The state fair happens once a year in the fall.

Activites

  • Jumping Pillow
  • Pig Races
  • Goat Shows
  • Carnival Rides
  • Outdoor Concerts
